<objective>
Build the thin verdict driver that turns the already-shipped measurement stack (close_loop,
replicate, bootstrap, judge, report) into a single pre-registered 4-contrast verdict. It runs
pack/memory/wiki/full each vs none, computes per-contrast paired-bootstrap CI + Cohen's d,
applies Holm-Bonferroni, and writes 22-VERDICT.md with per-arm quality + tax + compounding
curve + PASS/NULL per D-02. It adds NO new measurement primitives and NO reimplemented
statistics — it orchestrates and interprets.

Purpose: VERD-02 (run the paired design across 5 arms + compounding curve) and VERD-03 (report
quality AND tax, apply the pre-registered rules, accept null). The entire driver + Holm + report
is provable in --mode cheap (deterministic, free, no claude), so CI never spends.
Output: bench/verdict.py, a paired_bootstrap_p addition to bench/bootstrap.py, tests/test_verdict.py.
