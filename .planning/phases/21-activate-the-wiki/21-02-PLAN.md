<objective>
Activate the dormant Phase-11 semantic wiki layer in production behind an opt-in flag, and harden its `[semantic]`-absent degradation. This is the WIKI-04/WIKI-05 consumer half: the orchestrator passes the correct layer union when the flag is on, the default (flag off) stays byte-identical, and a missing embedder becomes a one-time warning rather than a crash.

Purpose: The wiki layer measured 0.825 ~ oracle 0.800 but no production caller ever passes include_layers with "wiki", so it never fires. This wires it on — carefully, because passing `{"wiki"}` alone would silently drop the other four standard layers (D-06).

Output: `ProjectPreferences.wiki_layer` flag, a single `_STANDARD_LAYERS` constant + union at orchestrator.py:254, a one-time degradation warning, and a byte-identity regression test.
</objective>
