<objective>
Promote the memory->wiki distiller from bench tooling to a production module, expose it as the explicit `flowstate distill` command, and add manifest-tracked staleness gating that mirrors `flowstate pack`. This is the WIKI-03 producer half: after a run you distill, and the next run's wiki layer reads the fresh corpus (the deferred-safe "end-of-run" interpretation, D-03).

Purpose: The proven-best context layer (distilled wiki, 0.825 ~ oracle 0.800) has a bench-only producer. Production has no way to generate the corpus. This plan gives it one, without duplicating the distiller logic and without auto-invoking it inside run_pipeline (D-03 fence).

Output: `flowstate/distiller.py` (production module), `bench/distiller.py` (re-export shim), a `kind="wiki"` manifest entry + `is_wiki_stale`, and the `flowstate distill` CLI command.
</objective>
